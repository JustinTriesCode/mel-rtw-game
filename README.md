Game 2: Equationista

- Status: Early Access

- How to Play:
  - Look at the four values/equations around the center image.
  - Press the Arrow Key or WASD Key corresponding to the side with the lowest number.
  - Scoring: You get 10 points for a correct answer on your first try. If you miss, you can try again for reduced points.
  - The Goal: Keep your streak alive! If the numbers all get too high, the board will reset to give you a fresh start.

- Controls
  - Up/Down/Left/Right or W/A/S/D to play.
  - ESC to return to the Main Menu at any time.
